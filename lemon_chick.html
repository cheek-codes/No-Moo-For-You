Lemon Herb Grilled Chicken

This lemon herb grilled chicken recipe is flavorful and healthy, and it's perfect for meal prep. You can serve it with your favorite sides, such as roasted vegetables, quinoa, or brown rice. Enjoy!

Ingredients:
  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1/4 cup fresh lemon juice
  • 2 tbsp. olive oil
  • 2 tbsp. fresh thyme leaves, chopped
  • 2 tbsp. fresh rosemary leaves, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
    Instructions:
  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the lemon juice, olive oil, thyme, rosemary, garlic, salt, and black pepper.
  3. Place the chicken breasts in a shallow dish and pour the marinade over the chicken. Let marinate for at least 30 minutes or up to 4 hours in the refrigerator.
  4. Remove the chicken from the marinade and discard the excess marinade.
  5. Grill the chicken for 6-8 minutes per side or until the internal temperature reaches 165Â°F.
  6. Let the chicken rest for 5 minutes before slicing or serving.
  7. Divide the chicken among four meal prep containers and let cool to room temperature before storing in the refrigerator.
