Burrito Bowl

Click me to change my theme ...

This dairy-free burrito bowl is a flavorful and nutritious meal that features brown rice, grilled chicken, black beans, fresh veggies, tangy pickled onions, and a creamy avocado dressing.

Ingredients:
  • 2 c cilantro lime rice
  • 2 chicken breasts, baked or grilled, shredded or cut
  • 1 tbsp chili powder
  • 1 tbsp ground cumin
  • 4 c shredded lettuce
  • 1 c canned corn, prepare according to package directions
  • 1 c canned black beans, drained and rinsed and heated
  • optional: 1 c pico de gallo, homemade or store-bought dollop dairy free sour cream
  • optional: garnishâ€“green onions, cilantro, guacamole, or sauteed bell peppers and onions.
    Instructions:
  1. After chicken has been heated and shredded or cut into smaller bites combine with cumin and chili powder. Set aside.
  2. Evenly divide lettuce among 4 bowls.
  3. Top each bowl with chicken, cooked corn, black beans, and rice.
  4. Add a dollop of dairy free sour cream, pico de gallo, and fresh chopped cilantro. Optional
  5. Serve immediately.
